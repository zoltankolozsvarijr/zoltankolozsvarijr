How To Install the Prototype

Please confirm the installation dialog, then press your Home button to see the installation progress. Note that the app's icon might appear on any of your Home screens.

Wait for Skyscanner to install, and then launch

Tap Cancel in the Untrusted Enterprise Developer popup

Open iPhone Settings, and pick General

Scroll down and pick Profiles & Device Management

Tap SKYSCANNER LTD from the Enterprise App list

Tap the blue Trust SKYSCANNER LTD button

Confirm by tapping the red Trust button

Press your Home button!

Tap here if you got an error message.

Installation Errors

The alert "Unable to Download App" is a generic error message and can have various reasons, for example:

  • UDID is not included in the provisioning profile

  • Device or iOS version is incompatible with the build

  • Device storage is full

  • Actual download error

  • App is over 50 MB and downloaded over a cellular connection

Unfortunately, iOS does not provide a detailed error log. Always make sure that your device is registered with HockeyApp. If you are unable to solve the issue, please contact the developer with your account and device details.
